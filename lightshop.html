Our story

Lightshop started with a simple frustration: online shopping felt cold. You'd scroll through static photos, read reviews written by strangers, and click "buy" hoping for the best. There was no way to ask questions, see the product in real life, or feel the energy of a great find.

We set out to fix that. By combining live video with real-time commerce, we created a space where sellers can show — not just tell — and where shoppers can engage, ask, and decide with confidence.

Today, thousands of creators and small businesses go live on Lightshop every day, reaching audiences who genuinely want what they're selling. And millions of shoppers tune in not just to buy, but to be entertained, informed, and delighted.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: April 11, 2026
Contents
1. What we collect 2. Browser storage (this app) 3. How we use your data 4. Who we share it with 5. Cookies & analytics 6. Your rights 7. Security 8. Contact us

1. What we collect

When you use Lightshop, we collect information you provide directly (like your name, email address, and payment information when you make a purchase), information generated by your use of our services (like your viewing history, purchases, and interactions with streams), and technical data (like your IP address, browser type, and device information).

In the default demo deployment, checkout card fields are not sent to a payment processor; see Terms → Purchases for the demo-checkout notice.

We collect only what we need to provide a great service. We don't buy or broker data about you from third parties.

2. Browser storage (this app)

This deployment stores certain data only on your device using the browser's localStorage API (nothing is sent to our servers unless you later connect a backend):

  • dl_users — demo accounts you create (email, hashed password placeholder, name, following list, and related preferences).
  • dl_session — which account is currently signed in.

Clearing site data in your browser removes this information. If you ship a production backend, replace or migrate this storage and update this section accordingly.

3. How we use your data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Lightshop platform
  • Process your orders and payments
  • Send you notifications about streams you follow
  • Personalise your discovery feed based on your interests
  • Detect fraud and keep our platform safe
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send you product updates (you can opt out at any time)

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers. We do not let advertisers target you based on your shopping behaviour on Lightshop.

4. Who we share it with

We share data with third parties only as necessary to run Lightshop — for example, with payment processors to complete your transactions, cloud providers to store our data, and analytics providers to understand how the platform is used.

If you make a purchase, the seller (host) receives your name and shipping address to fulfil your order. Hosts do not receive your payment details.

5. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how people use Lightshop. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality.

If you enable privacy-friendly analytics (for example Plausible) via site configuration, page views may be counted without cross-site tracking cookies; review your analytics provider's documentation.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; to restrict or object to how we process it; and to receive a copy of it in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@lightshop.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices — including encryption in transit and at rest — to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, but we take the protection of your information seriously and review our practices regularly.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, contact us at privacy@lightshop.com.
